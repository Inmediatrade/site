ABOUT US

Inmedia Trade Ltd, based in the UK, is a company formed by the union of international trader and marketing experts, business consultants with decades of experience, and professionals in sport and promotional event organization.

Our corporate structure features a dynamic organizational chart that adapts to client demands and needs. Inmedia Trade integrates specialized consultants, each with a minimum of 20 years of experience, and world-class partner companies to guarantee top-level services.

Our working model is both direct operational and, when required, a ghost model for service delivery.

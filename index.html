ABOUT US

Inmedia Trade Ltd, based in the UK, is a dynamic company formed by the synergy of international trade and marketing experts, seasoned business consultants, and accomplished professionals in sports and promotional event organization. With decades of combined experience, our team brings a wealth of knowledge to every client interaction.

Our corporate structure is designed for agility, featuring a flexible organizational chart that seamlessly adapts to client demands and evolving market needs. We pride ourselves on integrating specialized consultants, each boasting a minimum of 20 years of hands-on experience, and partnering with world-class companies to ensure the delivery of top-tier services.

Whether operating directly as your strategic partner or adopting a 'ghost model' for discreet service delivery, our commitment remains unwavering: to provide impactful solutions that drive your business forward.

Precious Metals & Refinery

INMEDIA TRADE boasts as Trader the exclusive JV with ACTIVA SAN AGUSTIN GOLD REFINERY, a Spanish-based company specialized in the refining of raw gold and the trading of bullion. With a strong commitment to transparency, quality, and compliance, we operate as a trusted player in the international precious metals market.

Our Core Activities

  • Bullion Trading: Wholesale trading of investment-grade bullion, including bars and coins, tailored to the needs of financial institutions, investors, and international clients.
  • Gold Refining: Utilizing modern refining technologies, we process raw gold into high-purity bullion. Our facilities guarantee precision, efficiency, and full traceability throughout the refining chain.
  • Raw Gold Acquisition: We purchase raw and unrefined gold directly from trusted suppliers, including mining companies and licensed traders. Our procurement process adheres to strict compliance standards to ensure ethical sourcing.
